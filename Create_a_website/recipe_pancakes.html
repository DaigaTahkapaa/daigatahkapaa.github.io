Pancakes

  • 30 min
  • Easy
  • 2

Fluffy pancakes topped with sweet clementine slices, juicy blueberries, and a dusting of powdered sugar for the perfect treat.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • ¾ cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 clementines, peeled and segmented
  • ½ cup fresh blueberries
  • 2 tbsp powdered sugar

Method

  • Step 1

    Preheat the Pancake Zone
    Warm up your favorite non-stick skillet over medium heat. Whisper sweet nothings to it—it's about to do some magic. Lightly grease the pan with butter or cooking spray.

  • Step 2

    Mix the Secret Dry Ingredients
    In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and a pinch of salt. Use a whisk (or a magic wand if you have one) to make sure everything is well combined.

  • Step 3

    Create the Potion (Wet Ingredients)
    In another bowl, combine milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla extract. Stir until it's smoother than your last pickup line.

  • Step 4

    Unite the Realms
    Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir just until combined. Lumps? No problem—they're the quirky cousins of smooth batter and give pancakes their fluff.

  • Step 5

    Summon the Pancakes
    Scoop about ¼ cup of batter onto the preheated skillet. Cook until bubbles form on the surface and the edges look set. Flip with confidence (a pancake senses hesitation). Cook the other side until golden brown.

  • Step 6

    Repeat and Stack
    Repeat the scooping and flipping until you have a glorious stack of golden pancakes. Ensure they're stacked with pride like a pancake skyscraper.

  • Step 7

    Prepare the Decorators
    While the pancakes cool slightly, artfully segment your clementines and wash the blueberries. Resist the urge to eat them all during this step. It’s harder than it sounds.

  • Step 8

    Arrange with Elegance
    Place your pancakes on a plate and top them with clementine segments and blueberries. Feel free to use your inner artist here—think of your plate as a canvas.

  • Step 9

    Snowfall of Sweetness
    Using a fine sieve, dust the pancakes generously with powdered sugar. Pretend you're summoning winter on a tropical island.

  • Step 10

    Admire and Devour
    Take a moment to admire your creation (snap a photo if you're feeling fancy). Then dig in with joy, because these pancakes are not just food—they’re a love letter to breakfast.
